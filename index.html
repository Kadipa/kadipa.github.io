I'm an AI/ML Engineer at Six Atomic and a PhD researcher at KMITL, where I focus on Knowledge Distillation and Computer Vision. I've spent 4+ years bridging research and production, publishing 3 IEEE papers while building real-world AI systems in fashion-tech and data engineering.

My work spans the full stack of AI: from designing mathematical algorithms for clothing pattern grading, to building RAG + LLM pipelines, to end-to-end data engineering on AWS. My Master's research under Asst Prof. Dr. Ukrit Watachareeruetai achieved 88% F1-score on plant nutrient deficiency classification using ResNet50.

Beyond engineering, I speak at Women in AI Myanmar and mentor at GDG Yangon's ML Paper Reading Club because the best way to understand something deeply is to teach it.

Publications

  • Banana Plant Nutrient Deficiencies Identification using Deep Learning

    1 - 4 June, 2023

    9th International Conference on Engineering, Applied Sciences, and Technology (ICEAST)

    Vientiane, Lao PDR

    Deep CNN classification of banana plant nutrient deficiencies across 9 classes — healthy and 8 deficiency types. Evaluated on two public datasets with augmentation strategies. Achieved ~88% F1-score.

  • Black Gram Plant Nutrient Deficiency Classification in Combined Images Using Convolutional Neural Network

    4-6 March, 2020

    8th International Electrical Engineering Congress (iEECON)

    Chiang Mai, Thailand.

    Classified 7 nutrient conditions in black gram using combined old + young leaf images. ResNet50 features fed into MLP outperformed SVM and logistic regression. Achieved 88.33% accuracy.

  • Classification of Nutrient Deficiency in Black Gram Using Deep Convolutional Neural Networks

    10-12 July, 2019

    16th International Joint Conference on Computer Science and Software Engineering (JCSSE)

    Pattaya, Thailand.

    Benchmarked deep CNN transfer learning for nutrient deficiency detection on 4,088 black gram leaf images across 7 treatment classes. ResNet50 outperformed block-based methods and human-reported baselines. Best model: ResNet50 at 65.44% accuracy.
