Hello, Nice to meet you :smile:

In July 2020, I completed my master's degree in Computational Intelligence Systems (CIS) from King Mongkut's Institute of Technology Ladkrabang (KMITL) under the supervision of [Asst Prof. Dr. Ukrit Watachareeruetai](https://sites.google.com/site/watchareeruetai/Home). My master thesis was focus on computer vision and deep learning. More specifically, my work was about plant nutrient deficiencies identification using deep learning. Before that, I earned my bachelor's degree in Knowledge Engineering at the University of Information Technology (UIT), Yangon. During my undergraduate studies, I participated in many activities such as Hackthon, Programming Contest, and volunteering. Also, I interned at Bindez Pte., Ltd and Vision to Motion (V2M) Myanmar Co.,Ltd. As a final year term project, I did word segmenatation tool for Myanmar language with Hnin Aye Lwin and Hus Yadana Win.
